About Me

I am a Master's graduate in Computer Science (Study Program: ICT for Smart Societies) from Politecnico di Torino, Italy. I am very interested in the research and development of Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Robotics, and VR/AR/MR.

I wrote my Master's thesis at the Department of Control and Computer Engineering, supervised by Prof. Alessandro Aliberti and Prof. Edoardo Patti. My thesis was published as a paper at the International Conference on Computers, Software, and Applications 2023. Before my Master's studies, I worked as a Robotics Engineer for two years at Kunshan Intelligent Equipment Research Institute, a subsidiary of Shenyang Automation Research Institute. Before that, I got my Bachelor's degree in Electronic Science and Technology with distinction from Southwest University, with two patents on intelligent devices.
