About Me

I am a Master's graduate in Computer Science (Study Program: ICT for Smart Societies) from Politecnico di Torino, Italy. I wrote my Master's thesis at the Department of Control and Computer Engineering, supervised by Prof. Alessandro Aliberti and Prof. Edoardo Patti. Before my Master's studies, I worked as an Electrical Control Engineer for two years at Kunshan Intelligent Equipment Research Institute, a subsidiary of Shenyang Automation Research Institute. Before that, I got my Bachelor's degree in Electronic Science and Technology with distinction from Southwest University. My interest lies in the intersection between Sensing, Signal Processing, and Applied Machine Learning.
